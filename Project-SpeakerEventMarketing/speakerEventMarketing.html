About the Project

TEDxCMU Fall Mini Event is TEDxCMU's main event of the semester. Speakers come together to share personal experiences and vulnerable insights. This year's line-up spoke about topics from life-changing haircut to a comedian's inner thought. Event featured free hot chocolate and donuts. These promotional material were created to spread the word about the event and its details across campus and over online presence.


Design Specifications

Format

The event required a main promotional poster to distribute across campus, as well as a teaser image that could be shared through social media before the announcement of the event.

Content: The design focused on conveying relevant information, including time, location, and additional details, while maintaining the TEDxCMU branding. The event required a main promotional poster.

Elements: Since the event is fall themed, the poster used elements such as fall flora and fauna and steaming cups of hot chocolate

Color Design: To comply with TEDxCMU identity, the color palette included Red, Yellow, and Blue. A pop of orange was also included to fit in with the fall theme.


Design Process

Individual elements were illustrated in Procreate. Photoshop was used for composition and typography.
